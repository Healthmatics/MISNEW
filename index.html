About

Various Activities To Keep Your Child Engage!

At Moonlight International School, we believe learning goes beyond the classroom. That’s why we offer a wide range of fun, interactive, and educational activities designed to stimulate your child’s mind and body. From music and dance, to science experiments, storytelling, drama, sports, arts and crafts — each day is filled with opportunities to explore, discover, and grow. Our carefully planned programs are designed to build confidence, creativity, teamwork, and a lifelong love for learning in every child.

  • Air Conditioned Class Rooms
  • Neat And Clean Wash rooms
  • Canteen Provides Healthy Foods For Kids
  • Large Play Areas For Children

Up Coming Events

Special Program Is Being Conduct For Settlers

March 14, 2024 10:00 am to 12:00 pm

We understand that starting a new school can be a big step — especially for young children. That’s why Moonlight International School runs a Special Settlers Program designed to help new learners adjust smoothly and confidently to their new environment. This gentle introduction includes short school hours, play-based activities, and extra attention from our warm, caring staff. Our goal is to make every child feel welcome, safe, and excited to begin their learning journey at their own pace.

Art And Craft Camp For Pre Schoolers

April 20, 2025 10:00 am to 12:00 pm

Our Art and Craft Camp is a colorful world where little hands create big imaginations! At Moonlight International School, we guide preschoolers through fun, age-appropriate art projects using safe materials like paper, glue, crayons, clay, and paint. From finger painting and paper cutting to making masks and collages, every activity helps improve fine motor skills, creativity, and self-expression. It's not just about making art — it’s about building confidence and having fun while learning through play!

Science Experiments And Lessons For Kids

April 01, 2025 10:00 am to 12:00 pm

At Moonlight International School, we make science exciting and accessible for our young learners! Our hands-on science lessons are designed to spark curiosity and develop critical thinking. Children get to explore simple, safe experiments like mixing colors, growing plants, making volcanoes, and learning about magnets and floating objects. These fun activities help them understand the world around them, ask questions, and enjoy learning through discovery. Science at our school is not just a subject — it’s an adventure!
